# Code of Conduct

`gha-matrix-scout` should be a practical and respectful place to collaborate.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, constructive, and specific.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, and behavior.
- Respect different experience levels, time zones, languages, and backgrounds.
- Give credit for others' work.
- Assume public project spaces are not appropriate for private or sensitive information.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or sustained personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruption after maintainers ask for a change in behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues or pull requests; or limit participation when behavior harms the project. Enforcement should be proportionate, documented when possible, and focused on restoring a useful working environment.
